## Tips

- Always end with a measurable or observable result, not just "and it went well."
- For failure stories, the "what you'd do differently" beat matters as much as
  the story itself — it shows reflection, not just recall.
- Reuse the same 5-6 stories across "tell me about a time..." questions rather
  than trying to have a unique story for every possible prompt.
