# Questions to Ask the Interviewer

Pick 2-3 based on who you're talking to (engineer vs manager vs skip-level).

## For engineers

- What does the on-call rotation actually look like day to day?
- What's the most annoying piece of tech debt on this team right now?
- How do code reviews typically go — fast rubber stamp, or deep discussion?

## For managers

- How is success measured for this role in the first 90 days?
- What's the team's biggest challenge right now?
- How does the team decide what to build next?

## For anyone

- What made you decide to join/stay at this company?
- What's something that surprised you after joining?

## Avoid

- Anything easily answered by the company website/public docs.
- Pure compensation/benefits questions this early (save for recruiter/HR conversations).
